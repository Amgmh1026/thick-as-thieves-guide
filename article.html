Prologue — Silent Heist

Mission walkthrough · Estimated time: 12–18 minutes

Overview

Short summary of goals and recommended loadout.

Step-by-step

  1. Enter the compound through the south gate, avoiding the patrol.
  2. Disable the alarm at the generator — use a silenced tool.
  3. Pick the lock to the vault using the blue trinket found in the guardroom.
  4. Escape via the hidden tunnel north of the courtyard.

Tips & Tricks

  • Use shadows to remain undetected during patrol changes.
  • Distract guards with a stone to the east side.
